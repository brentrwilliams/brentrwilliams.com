Hi! I'm Brent, a 24 year-old Technical Director living in Glendale, California. I currently work at DreamWorks Animation.

I received my B.S. and M.S. in Computer Science from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo in June of 2015, graduating in the top 10% of my class.

My passion and strongest skills are in computer graphics. At Cal Poly I took every graphics related course they offered, and was an instructional student assistant for two of them.

My work experience in computer graphics started with interning in the Production Services and Resources Department of Sony Pictures Imageworks. After that, I was hired by Apple to help support iOS game developers by creating example applications using Apple's graphics framework, Metal.

When not developing, and maintaining tools for artists at DreamWorks, you will likely find me on the sand, playing beach volleyball, or watching the latest 3D animated feature film.

If you would like to find out how to get in touch with me, see my contact page.
